Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)


What is Burst Beauty? How does it work?

Burst Beauty is an online beauty shop - a beauty collection, - whose mission is to bring the best price for all, especially for younger audiences. There are thousands of people who choose to buy their beauty products online, but what makes us different? Our aim is to make your shopping experience simple and affordable, with the best range of brands available. Burst Beauty is where you discover beauty products offered at shocking prices and share the happiness with your friends.

We are certified partners and distributors of the brands we carry so you'll only get 100% Authentic products delivered to you. As we are an online beauty store with no physical shops and overheads, you know we are passing great savings on to you, ensuring that you are offered only the best value in the comfort of your home!

Animal Testing Policy

All of the products in Burst Beauty are no longer tests any of its products or any of its ingredients on animals, anywhere in the world nor does its delegate this task to others. An exception could only be made if regulatory authorities demanded it for safety or regulatory purposes.

Yes, Burst Beauty is only available online and currently, no physical events or stores by us.
