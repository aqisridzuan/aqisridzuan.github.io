About Burst Beauty


Burst Beauty Makeup is all about selling the essentials in makeup products which is for face, eyes, lips and tools for all ages of makeup enthusiasts and beginners.
Burst Beauty is a reselling store with the most affordable prices. It does not own any physical store, as we considered it to be a huge advantage for us frankly as we make huge savings and we pass these savings straight onto customer ensuring with low prices. The Burst Beauty Makeup is commonly aimed especially for young women such as students.
The target audience for the website will largely be aimed at young women which is from 15 -30 as they are the target audience that we think would certainly be more attracted to our website. Younger women tend to have difficulties in finance, however they still intended to dress the best.
By Burst Beauty, we will guarantee on our customers that the products are 100% from the original brands yet having a better and lower prices that are affordable for all.

We are based in Malaysia and worldwide postage is available in all country.
